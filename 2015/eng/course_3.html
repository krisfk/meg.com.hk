Motorcycle Course

Besides private car and light goods vehicle, motorcycle is another beginner course. Motorcycle course is welcomed by the teenagers, due to the lower cost (course fee and the car) when comparing with light goods vehicle or private car.

Student could choose between manual and automatic transmission motorcycle course. No matter which type of transmission they choose, according to the Transport Department, all students must complete and pass the mandatory test at the government designated driving school before taking the on-road test, Students would be entitled the probationary driving license after passing the road test.
